The importance of Semiconductors

Moden electronic devices rely on semiconductors in order to function such as smartphones, computers, and even supersonic aircrafts. They are the foundation of modern digital world and the heart of today's AI data centers development. In addition, modern research and development efforts depend on access to the high-powered computing enabled by these advanced chips.

With the rise of AI, the demand of semiconductors will be higher. Then, many countries and companies are competiting to have the ownership of the technology of making advanced chips.
